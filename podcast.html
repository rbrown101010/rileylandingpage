New Vibe Podcast

As co-host of the New Vibe Podcast (@newvibepod), Riley Brown explores the intersection of artificial intelligence, creativity, and the future of work through engaging conversations with industry leaders, creators, and innovators.

"The New Vibe Podcast is where we dive deep into how AI is reshaping not just technology, but culture, creativity, and human potential."

The podcast serves as a platform for thoughtful discussion about the implications of AI advancement beyond just the technical aspects. Riley and his co-hosts examine how artificial intelligence is influencing various industries, creative processes, and societal structures.

Regular Topics

  • AI in Creative Industries

    Exploring how artists, writers, designers, and other creatives are incorporating AI into their workflows and what this means for the future of creativity.

  • The Future of Work

    Discussing how AI is changing job markets, creating new opportunities, and what skills will be valuable in an AI-driven economy.

  • Democratization of Technology

    Examining how AI tools are making complex capabilities accessible to everyday users and the broader implications of this trend.

  • Ethical AI Development

    Addressing the responsible development and deployment of AI technologies, including discussions about bias, privacy, and societal impact.

  • Startup and Innovation Stories

    Featuring conversations with entrepreneurs building AI-powered solutions and the challenges they face in bringing new technologies to market.

The podcast format allows for more nuanced exploration of topics that might only receive surface-level treatment in shorter social media content. Listeners gain deeper insights into the strategic thinking behind AI adoption and the real-world experiences of people navigating this technological shift.

Riley's approach on the podcast mirrors his educational philosophy - making complex topics accessible while maintaining depth and intellectual rigor. The show attracts both AI enthusiasts and curious newcomers looking to understand how artificial intelligence will shape their personal and professional futures.
