About Riley Brown

Riley Brown has emerged as the #1 AI educator on social media, transforming how people understand and interact with artificial intelligence. Based in the Greater Seattle Area, Riley is the co-founder of VibeCode and co-host of the New Vibe Podcast.

"My mission is to educate people on how to use AI tools that are either very cheap or free and use it to better communicate with the world digitally."

Riley's journey into AI education began with a simple realization: artificial intelligence doesn't have to be intimidating. Through his comprehensive tutorials and engaging social media presence, he has made AI accessible to millions of people who previously felt excluded from the technology revolution.

His flagship content, "The 6 Steps to Master AI," has been described as "the most comprehensive guide to AI currently available" and covers everything from basic chat AI tools to advanced coding applications, introducing over 100 practical ways to leverage AI without technical expertise.

Beyond education, Riley is pioneering the concept of "Vibe Coding" - the revolutionary approach to building applications using only AI, requiring no traditional coding knowledge. This methodology is transforming how we think about software development and democratizing app creation.

Riley's rapid rise in the AI education space is unprecedented. In just 45 days, he grew from zero to 201,000 followers on TikTok, establishing himself as a trusted voice in the AI community. His content spans multiple platforms, each tailored to deliver maximum value to his audience.
